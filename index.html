About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Télécom SudParis in the CITI research team.

Between 2017 and 2020, I was a PhD student working at Geprovas and Icube. I was under the supervision of Christophe Collet, Nabil Chakfé and Emmanuel Monfrini. I graduated from the French engineering school Télécom SudParis in 2017.

My research interests are artificial intelligence and machine learning techniques both on theoretical and applied aspects. I particularly enjoy building probabilistic models. I also work on applications of probabilistic models in medical imaging for vascular surgery.

Contact: hugo_gangloff(at)telecom-sudparis(dot)eu.
