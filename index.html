About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at IRISA in the OBELIX research team under the supervision of Sébastien Lefèvre, Minh-Tan Pham and Luc Courtrai. I am working on the unsupervised detection of animals in marine images.

Between 2020 and 2021, I was a postdoctoral researcher at Télécom SudParis in the CITI research team. There, I worked with Yohan Petetin and Katherine Morales on unsupervised statistical image segmentation approaches.

Between 2017 and 2020, I was a PhD student working at Geprovas and Icube. I was under the supervision of Christophe Collet, Nabil Chakfé and Emmanuel Monfrini. I worked on developping new tools for the processing of images from vascular surgery.

Before that, I graduated from the French engineering school Télécom SudParis in 2017.

Contact: hugo.gangloff(at)univ-ubs(dot)fr.
